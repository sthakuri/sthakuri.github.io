About Us

If you are currently a writer and work as a freelancer, we offer you the opportunity to expand your knowledge and potential by building your brand as an Author. We give full credit to our Authors and support and promote empowerment of women through our network. With risk comes reward, but we will handle the stressful side of the business. We will help you enjoy the benefits of earning income over time. Why would you limit yourself and the value of your work by accepting ghostwriting contracts to pay a small one-time fee? Freelancer has the word free in it because you are essentially selling your work at a minimal price compared to the value it can possess.

We look forward to helping you through the publishing process. Our knowledge will help you publish your first, second, or subsequent books. We sign every Author to a one-book contract to start to allow Authors flexibility in their individual careers. 

What We Publish

Travel, Education, Poetry, Fantasy, Action, Crime, both Fiction and Non - Fiction. Everything & Anything educational & factual. No hate or cancel culture promotion will be advertised or published with our services.

Our Mission

We publish woman Authors from around the world and allowing them to use our platform in order to share their message. . We help Authors make passive income. We are committed to helping you achieve your own personal success and growth by developing and expanding your brand. We would like to take the blind fold off your eyes to allow you to see your full potential. Is your goal to get paid a flat fee to write books and short stories and blogs , or is it to have a potential stream of income from the books you have written?
