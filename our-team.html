Our team

One of our missions is to promote Women's rights by allowing Women have a voice world-wide. We are committed to publishing works of female Authors from around the world. We have an Instagram account dedicated to womensâ€™ advancement by forming a strong network of talented women. Our Author team is a support group with the goal of individual success. This team is coordinated by Mark Tyhurst & Mariya Toncheva, based on a vision of offering a forum for authors and vehicle for publication of their works.. If you think you have what it takes to become an Author & you would like to join our team, please send us the first 100 words of your book.
